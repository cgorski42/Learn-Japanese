Additional Language Learning Resources:

Thank you for stoppign by and learning with us! Here are some additional resources to help you learn to speak Japanese in no time!

  • Japanese Pod 101

    Practice your listening skills through almost 3000 Japanese podcasts ranked by difficulty.

  • Jalup

    "Level Up" through a creative and well laid out system of objective that guide you as you make study plans and goals to achieve japanese excellence.

  • Textfugu

    A Japanese textbook focused on self-learners. The first section is free!

  • JLPT study

    A website with study guides and resources that focus on preparing for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test.

  • jgram

    Another good site to visit if you are preparing for the JLPT this site focuses on grammar patterns you'll need to excel in Japanese.

  • animelon

    Learn Japanese through anime! An easy way to learn while you relax. Allows you to slow playback speed and add japanese subtitles in Kanji and hiragana.
